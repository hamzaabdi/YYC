Canada Wheat Export Dashboard

Interactive chart showing Canadian wheat export volume by crop year (ending in July), based on Statistics Canada data.

Key Observations

  • Export volumes fluctuate annually, with notable surges in the early 2010s and again post-2020.
  • The 2020s show record-level exports, likely driven by increased global demand and improved yields.
  • Export volumes are calculated in thousands of metric tonnes. For example, 25,000 = 25 million tonnes.

TL;DR: Canadian wheat exports remain strong, with clear upward trends emerging in the 2020s. Stakeholders should monitor for sustainability and future demand shifts.
