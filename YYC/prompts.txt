Project Title: Calgary Supply Chain & Economic Insights

Goal: Build a visually stunning, data-driven website that positions me as a thought leader on Calgary’s economy and global supply chain disruptions. The site should combine evergreen insights with dynamic dashboards that update automatically when I upload new data. It must attract recruiters, employers, and professionals in Calgary’s business ecosystem.

Design Style:
	•	Clean, professional, modern (inspired by The Economist + World Bank dashboards).
	•	Neutral/dark theme with strong accent colors (blue, green, or gold).
	•	Infographics + charts integrated into every article.
	•	Responsive design for desktop + mobile.

Core Sections:
	1.	Homepage
	•	Headline: “Independent Insights on Calgary’s Supply Chains & Economy.”
	•	Subheadline: “Analyzing how global disruptions ripple into Calgary’s energy, agriculture, logistics, and aviation sectors.”
	•	Featured dashboards + previews of top articles.
	•	CTA: “Follow me on LinkedIn for weekly updates.”
	2.	Calgary’s Supply Chain & Economy Overview (Evergreen)
	•	SEO pillar page describing Calgary’s role in oil, gas, agriculture, aviation, and logistics.
	•	Permanent reference piece.
	3.	Energy & Fuel Supply Chains
	•	Dashboard: Brent Crude, WTI, Jet Fuel vs. CAD exchange rate.
	•	Static analysis of how fuel prices impact Calgary businesses.
	4.	Agriculture & Food Logistics
	•	Static insights: grain, beef, canola exports from Alberta.
	•	Dashboard: Wheat/Canola price trends + Alberta export volumes.
	5.	Aviation & Air Cargo (YYC Hub)
	•	Calgary International Airport’s role in cargo & global trade.
	•	Dashboard: YYC cargo throughput + shipping rates from Asia.
	6.	Business Supply Chain Risks 2025
	•	Static playbook: FX swings, inflation, container costs.
	•	Dashboard: CAD/USD exchange rates + Vancouver–Calgary freight costs.
	7.	Insights / Blog (Dynamic Weekly Posts)
	•	Example: “This week’s jet fuel spike and what it means for Calgary importers.”
	•	Funnel to LinkedIn for discussion.
	8.	Case Studies Library (Evergreen)
	•	Suez Canal blockage, COVID disruptions, Panama drought.
	•	Each case linked to how Calgary was affected.
	9.	About Me
	•	Diploma in Supply Chain Management (SAIT).
	•	BBA in General Management (in progress, University of Lethbridge).
	•	Lean Six Sigma Green Belt.
	•	Founder of SourceProof (supply chain verification).
	10.	Contact / Newsletter
	•	Simple signup form: “Get Calgary-focused supply chain insights straight to your inbox.”
	•	Option to connect via LinkedIn.

Dynamic Features:
	•	Embedded dashboards (Google Sheets → Looker Studio or Power BI).
	•	Dashboards update automatically when I upload fresh data.
	•	Tags & categories for SEO (“Energy,” “Agriculture,” “Logistics,” “Macroeconomics”).
	•	Social sharing + LinkedIn integration.

Tone & Content Style:
	•	Clear, data-driven, accessible.
	•	Blend static evergreen explainers with dynamic dashboards.
	•	Visual-first (charts > walls of text).
	•	Designed for Calgary businesses, recruiters, and professionals.
